The Challenge

The challenge was not merely to revamp the existing site of TISS University, but to transform it into a seamless digital platform that would elevate the user experience for students, professors, and all other stakeholders.
My objective was to transform the website into an intuitive platform characterized by streamlined and user-friendly interfaces.

User Survey & Research

I proactively connected with multiple students to discuss their site experiences, seeking insights into challenges and frustrations for pinpointing improvement areas. Collecting firsthand user data and noting pain-points aimed to tailor optimal solutions, ensuring proposed enhancements directly addressed user concerns for a more satisfying browsing experience.
